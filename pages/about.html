About Me

Hi, I’m Roshan Khadka, an 11th grade student from Gothatar, Kathmandu, Nepal.
I’m passionate about web design, branding, and content creation — blending technical precision with creativity to build stylish, responsive layouts.
Over the past year, I’ve been experimenting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript,
customizing Blogger templates, and exploring backend basics like Node.js.
I also enjoy working with Photoshop and Premiere Pro to design thumbnails, edit videos, and bring visual stories to life.

My goal is to create a professional online presence that reflects both my identity and my community.
I believe in learning by doing, troubleshooting every detail, and adapting modern design techniques to local realities.
This portfolio is a growing hub of my projects, skills, and creative journey.
In the future, I’ll be adding a dedicated section here to showcase my projects and creative experiments.
